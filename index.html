Crystals Collector

Rules

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals to the right. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number, you lose if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click ont it.

Each time the game starts, teh game will change the value of each crystal.
