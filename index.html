Game Rules

A TARGET NUMBER will be randomly selected at the beginning of each new game.

Your objective is to match the TARGET NUMBER by clicking on the crystals.

Each crystal holds a hidden value which will be added to the PLAYER SCORE.

The value for each crystal will not change during the game, however, it may change from round to round.

You WIN by matching the PLAYER SCORE to the TARGET number without going over.

 

GOOD LUCK - and happy crystal collecting!
